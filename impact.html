SPECIAL NEEDS

The Methodist Church has always held a view of a ministry of education, and no child is left behind. ACSF funds learning support through additional specialist staffing, customized training and tailored materials.

Approximately 35% of our boys at primary level only have one or more diagnosed learning or behavioural challenges.

STUDENT ENRICHMENT

We provide supplementary support for Student Academic, Cultural and Co-curricular Activities (CCA) and Skills Enhancement to supplement core so as to provide our students with greater diversity and quality of resources.

SOCIAL COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Through ACSF, donor gifts reach student-led Community Welfare Initiatives that provide seed funding for local community outreach projects. Since our inception, ACSF has disbursed over $500,000 to student organizations in the 6 ACS schools in Singapore in support of their respective Values In Action (VIA) initiatives with over 50 partner Volunteer Welfare Organizations (VWOs).
