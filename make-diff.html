The Endowment Fund

Donors who do not require tax deductibility are encouraged to donate to the Endowment Fund in order to grow its balance in the long term. Over time, the income from this endowment will achieve the long-term goal of generating the financial resources needed to ensure the sustainability of school-initiated, faculty and alumni-focused programs.

The IPC Fund

Donors to the IPC Fund qualify for tax deduction under the prevailing tax provisions. These are restricted funds and gifts to the IPC fund must be disbursed to an IPC within 5 years of receipt. The immediate beneficiaries of our IPC Fund are the ACS schools in Singapore for programs which support or enhance an education that promotes certain qualities and values.

Planned Recurring Gifts or Pledge

A recurring gift is an easy, automatic way to support the us with a continuous and dependable source of funding. Choosing to give recurring gifts or enables you to make a more generous gift than thought possible by spreading the gifts out over a longer period of time. To set up a recurring automated deduction (or GIRO), you can visit our secure online giving page and choose the recurring option via our Recurring Gift Authorisation Form. A pledge is a signed and dated commitment to make a gift by paying over a period of time, allowing you to spread payments and tax benefits over a period up to 5 years (typically this type of donation can often allow you to make a larger commitment). We will create a document for you that properly addresses your giving intent
